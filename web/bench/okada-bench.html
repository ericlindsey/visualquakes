SimpleQuakes - Okada (1985) in a WebGL2 fragment shader

Proof of concept: the full nonlinear Okada kernel runs once per pixel on the GPU. Drag a slider and the fringes redraw from updated uniforms - no geometry rebuilt. Serve this folder over HTTP (python -m http.server) so ES modules and reference.json load.
